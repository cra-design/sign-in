CRA account help

What to do if your CRA account becomes locked, and how to avoid getting locked out.

On this page

  • Recover a locked account
  • Avoid getting locked out

Recover a locked account

Your account will become locked if you enter the wrong information too many times for any of the following:

  • CRA user ID or password
  • One-time passcode for multi-factor authentication (MFA)
    You do not need to call if you are only temporarily locked out
  • Security questions
  • CRA security code
  • The document verification service

To regain access to your account, you can either:

  • Go through the registration process again with new sign-in information
  • Contact us to have your account unlocked

Avoid getting locked out

Use the following strategies to avoid getting locked out of your account.

Set up your CRA user ID and password

Update your multi-factor authentication (MFA) settings

Choose memorable security questions
